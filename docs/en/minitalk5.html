Polishing up and Preparing to Finish

Referring from last week . . . .

What you need to finish during beta

  • ALL mechanics
  • ALL content extensions
  • Most sound effects
  • Most art and UI

For those who did not make it for previous weeks' targets

  • No polishing phase for you (EA Games, anyone?)
  • Finish up what you can

Gold Phase Tasks

  • Final polishing on art, sound and UI
  • Playing whack-a-mole with bugs
  • Making promotional material
    • Trailer/screenshots
    • Poster/title art

For those who are really attached...

If you can’t move on, just imagine this is graded and has a hard deadline. If you really like your game, you can expand on it after the workshop. But get it to a ‘finished’ state first! It may never move on.
