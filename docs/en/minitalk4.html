Time to Do Some Cutting (not the psycho kind)

Referring from last week . . . .

What you CANNOT leave until Beta Stage

  • Main gameplay mechanics
  • Basic art assets
  • Essential UI

Cut, cut, cut!

"Oh shit we can't make it" — Everyone ever.
Time to trim your dreams before you get rekt.

Assess your features

  • Completed and good enough?
  • Needs polishing or fixing?
  • Not done??
  • Not even started????

(I hope you started already. Really.)

What will it look like next week?

  • Completed and good enough?
  • Needs polishing or fixing?
  • Not done??
  • Not even started????

What you need to finish during beta

  • ALL mechanics
  • ALL content extensions
  • Most sound effects
  • Most art and UI

Only leave for GOLD,

  • Final polishing on art & UI
  • Playing whack a mole with bugs.
  • Making promotional materials
    • Trailer / screenshots
    • Poster / title art

For those who are really attached...

If you can’t move on, just imagine this is graded and has a hard deadline. If you really like your game, you can expand on it after the workshop. But get it to a ‘finished’ state first! It may never move on.

Now,

Git on with it.
